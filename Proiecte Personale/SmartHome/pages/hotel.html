KNX solutions for hotels
The best KNX solutions for the management of accommodation facilities of all sizes. Maximum comfort for your guests, combined with a simple and complete management. Also for facilities without local reception.
What can you do with Home Automation in the Hotel?
In modern reception facilities there is an increasing need to offer new comforts to customers and at the same time to optimize the resources used, limiting consumption as much as possible.
Centralized management
From one or more locations, even outside the structure, you can check in real time the presence of your guests and the room status, including calls and alarm signals.
Comfort and savings
The system will manage the temperature of each room independently, setting it to offer maximum comfort to your customers and obtain maximum cost savings.
Image return
A modern and comfortable structure guarantees customer satisfaction and a remarkable image return on advertising and booking platforms.
