Bticino: aesthetic research and smart technology
Bticino home automation devices, developed with the international KNX standard, are a guarantee of complete and high-performance solutions that allow you to implement a flexible and open system.
KNX Devices and Applications
The Bticino Knx offer combines performance, aesthetics and comfort to meet the needs of different customers both in the residential and tertiary sectors. The Knx controls allow you to manage the lighting (On/Off, Dimmer, Scenarios, Motorizations), the shutters (up, down, stop), the curtains and other devices. The numerous Knx sensors in the Bticino catalogue allow advanced automation functions to be implemented and to guarantee the best energy performance. Thanks to the different types of controllers and actuators it is possible to control multiple types of loads (On/Off ignitions, dimming, DALI technology, energy consumption measurement, etc.).
Living Now: the civil series dedicated to home automation
Living Now is the brand new Bticino home automation wiring accessories series that is characterized by clean design and the rigor of the geometries. Its unique and distinctive shape has been designed to fit all homes.
An innovative design
The Living Now push-button panels seem to float on the wall, The innovative "floating frame" element allows the plaque to adapt to the irregularities of the walls and therefore hide their imperfections. Thanks to the "full touch" controls, the ergonomics of the button is completely revolutionized. The light can be turned on or off by pressing anywhere on the key surface. After being pressed, the keys return to their original position, perfectly aligned.
Colours and finishes
The keys, or covers, are treated with a finely satin finish that is matte to the eye and pleasantly silky to the touch. They are available in three different colors: white, sand and black. The aesthetic side finishes or plates are available in three materials (technopolymer, zamak and wood) and sixteen different finishes to adapt to any taste or style of living.
