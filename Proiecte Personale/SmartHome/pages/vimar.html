Vimar Knx: positive energy since 1945
Since 1945 Vimar has been working with passion and desire to innovate: a story that began in the immediate post-war period and that today sees the company present all over the world.
Standard Knx
Vimar offers quality and design solutions within everyone's reach, offering a range of KNX devices in continuous technical and aesthetic evolution. The great advantage of the KNX system is that it allows the supervision of all functions and the centralized management of the building: lighting, heating and cooling, management of security systems (alarm, burglar alarm and cameras) monitoring of gas / electricity consumption.
Applications
In the KNX panorama, Vimar is probably the company most oriented towards residential and above all hotel solutions, with a complete and design access control system.
Made in Italy
Even today, Vimar continues to produce in Italy, at the Marostica headquarters, because it believes in Made in Italy, combining aesthetic quality, technological innovation and ease of installation.
Buttons and thermostats are created with the use of precious materials and selected finishes and marry square corners and rigorous lines that integrate perfectly into any environment, creating an original soul every time.
