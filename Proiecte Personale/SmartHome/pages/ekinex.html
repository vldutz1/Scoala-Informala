Ekinex home automation rewrites the rules of design
Ekinex devices are developed and manufactured in Italy. They are characterized by design, attention to detail, cutting-edge technology and innovation.
The idea behind the design of the devices is that of a design that is not pure aesthetic exercise, but includes, as fundamental elements, ease of use, immediate understanding of the function and the balanced and intelligent application of technology.
Ekinex and Konnex standard
Ekinex is developed on thebasis of the KNX standard, a system that complies with national, European and international regulations. The entire product range is used for building automation: private homes, offices, hotels, public environments.
Ekinex: Konnex standard and Made in Italy
Ekinex is an Italian company founded with the intention of creating a line of objects that would enclose the very essence of Made in Italy: attention to detail, minimal and elegant design and technological innovation. Ekinex products are an expression of Italian tradition revisited in a modern perspective and have been designed for a global market. Also for this reason all Ekinex push-button panels and home automation devices work on KNX protocol, the only globally recognized standard for home automation and building automation. Ekinex also supports the idea of a design that is not pure aesthetic exercise, but is also based on ease of use and immediate understanding of the functionality of its devices, to make them easy and immediate to use for everyone. The product range is divided into three different series, consisting of buttons, thermostats and multifunction touchscreens, designed to meet every application need. The devices are available both in high-quality materials for exclusive environments or even in plastic materials for less demanding situations. All, indistinctly, bring colors and shapes that integrate harmoniously in every setting and in every situation.
