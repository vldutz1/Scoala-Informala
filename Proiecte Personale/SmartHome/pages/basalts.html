Basalts: technology and design come together.
Basalte home automation devices are developed and manufactured in Belgium in line with the international KNX standard for home automation systems.
Basalt's home automation push-button panels are characterized by their intuitive and elegant design. Simplicity and ease of use, combined with high quality materials distinguish the company on the market. Exclusive and timeless, they are designed to create a place where people can truly feel at home. Basalte designs minimal products, which can be inserted in any environment.
Solid and durable
Like the rock from which Basalte takes its name, all products have exceptional durability and resistance. They are meticulously designed and handmade and thanks to their harmonious, instinctive and contemporary look they integrate perfectly into any home.
The technical features that make Basalt unique
Basalt's push-button panels integrate natively with the KNX standard, the only internationally recognized standard for home automation. All push-button panels are equipped with an internal temperature sensor that measures the temperature of the environment. In addition, all the push-button panels of the Sentido series have multi-touch technology, which makes the entire surface of the control panel sensitive to touch, and a multichromatic LED backlight: the color of the light feedback changes according to the command implemented. In addition, the standby backlight makes it easy to use and locate in the dark. Each touch button can be freely programmed as desired, both for switching lights on and off, for moving shutters and blinds, and for launching customized scenarios.
